Welcome to the SmartEnzymeDB Database!

Introduction

The aim of the SmartEnzymeDB database is to provide a comprehensive collection of experimentally measured binding affinity data for all biomolecular complexes deposited in the Protein Data Bank (PDB). It provides an essential linkage between the energetic and structural information of those complexes, which is helpful for various computational and statistical studies on molecular recognition, drug discovery, and many more.

The SmartEnzymeDB database was originally developed by Prof. Lujia Zhang's group at the East China Normal University in China, which was first released to the public in September, 2024. The SmartEnzymeDB database is updated on an annual base to keep up with the growth of the Protein Data Bank.
